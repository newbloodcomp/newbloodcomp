5 Pillars Around the World
A New Blood Awards 2020 Submission

By Jonathan Goodrich, Katelyn Jepsen, Lily Pashapour, and Robert Schlatter

Short Animations: Check-in, baggage claim, small displays

Skyline Swap

This animation depicts various famous city skylines and architecture: New York City, Dubai, Tokyo, and London. It captures the ideas of trade and commerce and diversity and inclusion. It also exhibits a sense of togetherness and has international appeal.

Art

Here, we're trying to equate different art styles that have varying histories and are created in different contexts as both being works of art of equal value: graffiti and fine art. This animation portrays the ideas of diversity and inclusion and education. It could be produced in many different languages, thus giving it more international appeal.

Long Animation: Moving sidewalks, large displays

Around the World

With the transitions between scenes depicting various aspects of our world, we are able to capture all five pillars of HSBC: Sustainability, trade and commerce, education, diversity and inclusion, and sport. The interconnectedness of its style evokes a feeling of togetherness, and it has appeal internationally because it includes scenes from around the world.
